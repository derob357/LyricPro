## Part 5: Prize Distribution Strategy

### Option A: Top 3 Teams Split (Recommended)
- **1st place team:** 60% of prize pool
- **2nd place team:** 30% of prize pool
- **3rd place team:** 10% of prize pool

**Example (3-player team, $5/player, $1.35 prize pool):**
- 1st place: $0.81 per player
- 2nd place: $0.405 per player
- 3rd place: $0.135 per player

### Option B: Winner-Take-All
- **1st place team:** 100% of prize pool
- **2nd-3rd place:** $0

**Pros:** Simpler, higher incentive for top teams
**Cons:** Only 1 team wins, others feel disappointed

### Option C: Tiered by Score
- **Perfect score (all correct):** 100% of pool
- **90% correct:** 60% of pool
- **80% correct:** 30% of pool
- **Below 80%:** $0

**Pros:** Rewards skill, not luck
**Cons:** More complex calculation
